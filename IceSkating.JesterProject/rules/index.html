Required Officials

For every speed skating competition, you need a Referee and one Assistant Referee One or two Starters and an Assistant. A Finishing Line Judge, Chief Timekeeper and Assistant Chief Timekeepers. Lap Scorers, Track Judges, including two Corner Judges. One to observe the crossing of the lines in the finishing straight. and one Crossing Controller. You also need an Ice Technical Expert, and a Sport Expert

Timing

There's automatic and manual timings.
